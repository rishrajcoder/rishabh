    • Scenario Description

    As many people loose their life due to the unavailability of the required blood during the time of requirement, to address this we came up with the idea of a centralised database which is “The Redeemer – ABSMS”. With this project, required blood will reach the needy person in time and his life can be saved.

    Public Awareness

    We did a survey throughout our college in bengaluru and hardly anyone knows about any blood banks available in the city. This creates a lot of problem in the time of emergency situation and hence our idea is really beneficial for the society.
